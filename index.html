About Me

I am Mansi Goel, a passionate Ph.D. Scholar at CoSyLab, IIIT-Delhi, working under Prof. Ganesh Bagler. My research focuses on leveraging Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and Data Science to revolutionize the food domain. I work on structuring and analyzing vast datasets, predicting ingredient interactions, and developing AI-powered solutions for computational gastronomy.
